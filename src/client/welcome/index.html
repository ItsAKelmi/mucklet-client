Welcome to Wolfery!

What is this?

A textual world of roleplay. Create a character, wake them up, and join in.

Is it a roleplay chat?

Yes. But it has more in common with the textual MUCK games of old, where you explore and create a world for you to roleplay in.

Is there an age limit?

You must be at least be 18 to register. Any type of roleplay is allowed, including sexual roleplay. Some characters may also have explicit descriptions or images.

How to get started?

Click the button below, register an account, and create a character. Once inside the realm, you can always find friendly people willing to help you out.
