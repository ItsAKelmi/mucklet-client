What is this?

A textual world of roleplay. It is still in the making, but you are welcome to create a character, wake them up, and join in.

Is it a roleplay chat?

Yes and no. Here you you can explore and create lasting worlds for you and others to roleplay in.

Is there an age limit?

You must be at least be 18 to register. Most types of roleplay is allowed.

How to get started?

Click the button below, register an account, and create a character. Once inside the realm, you are free to create, explore, and roleplay.
